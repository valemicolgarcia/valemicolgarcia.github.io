Project Description

As a Research Fellow at the LEICI Institute (UNLP), I work on an applied research project that analyzes physiological signals during exercise and stress episodes in people with type 1 diabetes. This project combines signal processing techniques, data analysis, and machine learning to better understand how physiological systems respond under different conditions.

Project Objectives

  • Real-time analysis of physiological signals
  • Pattern identification during exercise and stress
  • Application of ML techniques for classification and prediction
  • Contribution to medical research in type 1 diabetes

Institutions

LEICI Institute - Universidad Nacional de La Plata (UNLP)

Role: Research Fellow (since May 2025)

Impact

This project demonstrates my ability to apply machine learning to real scientific research problems, working with complex data and contributing to advances in the health field.

Note: This is an ongoing research project. Specific details and results are subject to academic confidentiality.
